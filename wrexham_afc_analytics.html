Complete Hollywood era timeline
Sep 2020
Bid announced. Reynolds & McElhenney reveal interest in buying Wrexham AFC. World treats it as a celebrity prank.
Nov 2020
98.6% vote YES. Supporters Trust backs the Hollywood takeover. Deal valued at just £2 million.
Feb 2021
Takeover complete. RR McReynolds Company LLC completes acquisition. Reynolds and McElhenney become co-chairmen.
Jul 2021
Phil Parkinson appointed as manager. TikTok named shirt sponsor. Paul Mullin signed on a free from Cambridge United.
Oct 2021
First game attended. Reynolds & McElhenney watch Wrexham lose 3–2 to Maidenhead in their first live experience at the Racecourse.
May 2022
Double heartbreak. Wrexham finish 2nd with 88 pts but lose to Grimsby 5–4 in playoff SF (extra time). Also lose FA Trophy Final to Bromley at Wembley.
Aug 2022
'Welcome to Wrexham' premieres on FX/Hulu. Global viewing figures explode. Club reaches 181.3m Twitter impressions in the year.
Jan 2023
FA Cup 4th Round. Only non-league club to reach this stage. ESPN's most-watched football match globally that week. Beat Coventry and Sheffield Utd.
Apr 2023
PROMOTION 1. Clinch National League title with 107 pts, +68 GD — both division records. 15-year exile from the EFL ends. Reynolds: "I'm never sleeping again."
Jun 2023
Alpine F1 investment. Reynolds and McElhenney invest in Alpine F1 team alongside RedBird and Otro Capital as part of a £172m consortium deal.
Apr 2024
PROMOTION 2. League Two runners-up (88 pts) behind Stockport County. Back-to-back promotions. Club value estimated at ~£100m — 50× the purchase price.
Mar 2025
Record revenue revealed. £26.7m turnover in first EFL season — a record for a League Two club. Loans from R&M fully repaid by the club.
Apr 2025
PROMOTION 3 — Historic. Beat Charlton 3–0. Become the first club in English football history to win three consecutive promotions across the top five divisions. Championship bound after 43 years.
Aug 2025
Championship debut. First 2nd-tier season since 1981-82. Home opener draws worldwide media coverage. Pre-season included Australia/New Zealand tour.
Dec 2025
Apollo Sports Capital stake. US private equity firm buys minority stake. Club valued at approximately £150m — a +7,400% return in under 5 years.
Mar 2026
Now. 6th in the Championship with 54 pts from 34 games. Playoff contention. FA Cup 5th Round included Chelsea at Racecourse Ground. Revenue £33m. The impossible dream continues.
